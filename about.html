About me

Revenue Manager from Lisbon, Portugal

I was born in Lisbon, Portugal. Beautiful country on the South of Europe. I have studied and specialized myself in Hotel Management and have more than 18 years of experience in the Travel & Hospitality Industry. Started in the Sales & MICE department, continued with Front Desk and Operations, and now totally focused on REVENUE MANAGEMENT. Fortunate for being able to grow professionally in several countries and know different cultures. Left Portugal after University, lived my adult professional life mainly in Spain and Dominican Republic. Now I'm currently in New York, USA. Although my begginings were in the Hospitality Industry, lately I have put my focus on CODING & WEB DESIGN and I am loving this world.

My biggest motivation is based on learning something new every day.
